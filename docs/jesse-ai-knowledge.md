## Ideas and point of view

Jesse believes AI becomes useful inside organizations when it can act with the right context, not
merely generate fluent text. That context includes relationships, history, intent, provenance,
permissions, ownership, and prior decisions. Prompts alone are not organizational infrastructure.

He is especially interested in context graphs, human-agent collaboration, AI-native organizations,
and the one-person company as a systems-design problem. A solo builder does not simply need more
tools; they need an operating system that decides what work exists, what context agents receive,
and where human judgment stays in control.
